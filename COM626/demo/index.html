World and Eye Coords Demo

There are three boxes, a red box at (0,0,5), a green box at (5,0,0) and a blue box at (-5,0,0). Try moving around the scene to explore how the world position of the camera, and the eye coordinates of the boxes, changes.

Keys: w=forward, s=back, a=left, d=right, mouse=rotate camera, q=up, z=down.
